Begin by choosing a location and program to find the organizations that serve orphans and vulnerable children throughout Haiti. Refine your search results by using any combination of filters and keywords. For example, recommended keywords include organization name, city, street.

Made With Collaboration In Mind

The purpose of the website is to increase access to and coordination of programming and services for vulnerable children across all main service areas (i.e. health, education and legal services).

To accomplish our goal, this website provides an online directory of resources and services that can be easily searched with the use of an interactive online map of these services.

The immediate objective is to develop an online interactive map that: focuses on the identification of the services; includes all services identified and verified concerning the welfare of children; provides access to all stakeholders via a platform based on the Web; be available to the Bridgade IBESR phone service to provide operators with information specific reference.
